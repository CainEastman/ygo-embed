Blue-Eyes White Destiny Structure Deck Guide

The [[Blue-Eyes White Dragon]] is one of, if not, the most well known card in all of Yu-Gi-Oh. Even people who haven't played the game in a decade know of its fearsome strength and legendary status.

This deck focuses on summoning the powerful [[Blue-Eyes White Dragon]] and its various upgraded forms. It also includes essential support cards like [[Maiden of White]], [[Spirit with Eyes of Blue]], and [[Sage with Eyes of Blue]].

Key Synchro Monsters

The deck relies on powerful Synchro monsters like [[Blue-Eyes Spirit Dragon]] and [[Azure-Eyes Silver Dragon]] to control the board and protect your dragons.

Modern Staples

The structure deck also includes powerful meta staples such as [[Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring]] and [[Infinite Impermanence]] that can fit into almost any deck!
